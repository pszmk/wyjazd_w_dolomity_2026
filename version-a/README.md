## Quick summary

- **Car:** Compare [DiscoverCars](https://www.discovercars.com/) + [Rent.it](https://www.rent.it/autonoleggio/italia-1/bergamo-34/bergamo_aeroporto_orio_al_serio_bgy-42/) — **Locauto / Sicily by Car at BGY** often cheaper than Hertz/Sixt; city locals only if >€50 saving ([details](BUDGET.md#1-car-hire--bergamo-bgy--local-alternatives)).
- **Campsites:** Iseo (**arrival camp**) → **Colfosco** (~8 km to Gran Cir) → Olympia (eastern hikes).
- **Via ferrata:** Gran Cir (2 Sep), Ra Gusela (4 Sep) — grade A, beginner-friendly.
- **Hikes:** Seceda (3 Sep), Tre Cime (5 Sep). Braies/Cinque Torri optional extras.
- **Book now:** Colfosco, Olympia, Iseo (31 Aug), Tre Cime parking for **5 Sep**.
