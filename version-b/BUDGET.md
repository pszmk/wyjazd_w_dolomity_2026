## 2. Camping (6 nights, 2 sites)

| Camp | Nights | Low | High | Book |
|------|--------|-----|------|------|
| Camping del Sole, Iseo | 2 | €100 | €160 | [campingdelsole.it](https://www.campingdelsole.it/en/) |
| Camping Brione, Riva | 4 | €220 | €340 | [campingbrione.com](https://www.campingbrione.com/EN/) |
| **Subtotal** | **6** | **€320** | **€500** | |

**5 people:** confirm **large pitch** or two adjacent pitches; Brione and del Sole are busiest — book early for **3–6 Sep** (Brione) and **1–2 Sep** (del Sole).
