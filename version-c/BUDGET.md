## 2. Camping (6 nights, 3 sites)

| Camp | Nights | Low | High | Book |
|------|--------|-----|------|------|
| Camping del Sole, Iseo | 2 | €100 | €160 | [campingdelsole.it](https://www.campingdelsole.it/en/) |
| Camping La Rocca, Manerba | 1 | €50 | €80 | [laroccacamp.it](https://www.laroccacamp.it/) |
| Camping Brione, Riva | 3 | €165 | €255 | [campingbrione.com](https://www.campingbrione.com/EN/) |
| **Subtotal** | **6** | **€315** | **€495** | |

**5 people:** confirm **large pitch** or two adjacent pitches at each site. Book early for **1–2 Sep** (del Sole), **3 Sep** (La Rocca), **4–6 Sep** (Brione).

**Backup camp (Manerba):** [Camping Il Faro](https://www.campingilfaro.it/en) — similar price range if La Rocca is full.

## 3. Activities

Colodri ferrata is **in the main plan** (Day 5) — not optional.

| Item | Low | High | Notes |

**No cable cars** in this corridor — much cheaper activity line than Dolomites Core.

## 5. Other fees

| Fee | Typical cost | When |
|-----|--------------|------|
| CDW / excess reduction | €0–50 total | Optional at car pickup — read Italian franchise (franchigia) |
| Extra driver | €0–30 | If second person drives |
| ZTL fines | €80+ if unlucky | Don't drive into historic cores (Salò, Riva, Arco) — park outside |
| Late car return | €25+/hour | Align flight with rental return time |
| Tourist tax (tassa di soggiorno) | ~€1–1.50/person/night | Collected at camps — included in summary above |
| Monte Isola ferry (backup) | ~€9–11/person RT | Day 2 weather option only |
